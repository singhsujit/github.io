About Me

An accomplished academician with over 23 years of experience in teaching, research, and academic administration

Dr. Sujit Singh holds a Bachelor's in Production Engineering and Management from NIT Jamshedpur, a Master's in Industrial Engineering from IIT Delhi, and a Ph.D. in Manufacturing Management/Operations Management from the University of Malaya.

With expertise in Operations Management, Sustainable Manufacturing, Quality Engineering, and Optimization, Dr. Singh has published extensively in national and international journals and serves as a reviewer for high-impact publications.
