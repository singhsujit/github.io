About Me

An accomplished academician with over 23 years of experience in teaching, research, and academic administration

Professional Summary

Dr. Sujit Singh is an accomplished academician with over 23 years of experience in teaching, research, and academic administration. He holds a Bachelor's in Production Engineering and Management from NIT Jamshedpur, a Master's in Industrial Engineering from IIT Delhi, and a Ph.D. in Manufacturing Management/Operations Management from the University of Malaya, a globally ranked institution.

Dr. Singh has been actively involved in prestigious research centers such as CRDT at IIT Delhi and CPDM at the University of Malaya and was awarded an ICSSR research project by the Indian government.

Throughout his career, he has published extensively in national and international journals and serves as a reviewer for high-impact publications like the International Journal of Production Research and the Journal of Intelligent Manufacturing. His teaching career spans several prominent institutions, both in India and abroad, including a role as Associate Professor at the Ethiopian Technical University. Dr. Singh has also held leadership positions, including Program Coordinator and Head of the Mechanical Engineering Department.

He is an Institution of Industrial Engineers and ISTE member, with expertise in Operations Management, Sustainable Manufacturing, Quality Engineering, and Optimization. He is an Associate Professor at Woxsen University's School of Management in Hyderabad, India.
